Explanations:
CalendarQuarter: Refers to the quarter of the calendar year. A calendar year is typically divided into four quarters:
Q1: January 1 - March 31
Q2: April 1 - June 30
Q3: July 1 - September 30
Q4: October 1 - December 31
In your dataset, 1 indicates the first quarter of the calendar year.

FiscalQuarter: Refers to the quarter of the fiscal year. A fiscal year is a 12-month period used for financial reporting and can start on any date depending on the organization. The fiscal quarters are usually:
Q1: First three months of the fiscal year
Q2: Second three months
Q3: Third three months
Q4: Fourth three months
In your dataset, 3 indicates the third quarter of the fiscal year.

FiscalSemester: Refers to a six-month period within the fiscal year. There are typically two semesters in a fiscal year:
Semester 1: First six months of the fiscal year
Semester 2: Last six months of the fiscal year
